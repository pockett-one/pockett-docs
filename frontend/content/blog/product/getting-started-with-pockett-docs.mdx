# Getting Started with Pockett Docs

Welcome to Pockett Docs! This guide will help you get started with our powerful document management platform designed to streamline your workflow and improve productivity.

## What is Pockett Docs?

Pockett Docs is a comprehensive document management solution that helps businesses organize, track, and analyze their documents efficiently. Whether you're managing invoices, contracts, or project documentation, Pockett Docs provides the tools you need.

## Key Features

### 1. Document Organization
Organize your documents into projects and folders with ease. Our intuitive interface makes it simple to keep everything in order.

### 2. Real-time Insights
Get valuable insights into your document usage patterns, helping you make data-driven decisions about your document management strategy.

### 3. Connector Integration
Seamlessly connect with Google Drive and other cloud storage providers to centralize your document management.

### 4. Advanced Search
Find documents quickly with our powerful search functionality that understands context and content.

## Getting Started

### Step 1: Create Your Account
Sign up for a free account to get started. The process takes just a few minutes.

### Step 2: Connect Your Storage
Link your Google Drive account or other cloud storage providers to start importing your documents.

### Step 3: Organize Your Documents
Create projects and folders to organize your documents according to your workflow.

### Step 4: Explore Insights
Use the Insights dashboard to understand how your documents are being used and identify opportunities for improvement.

## Best Practices

- **Regular Organization**: Set aside time weekly to organize and categorize new documents
- **Use Tags**: Leverage tags to make documents easier to find
- **Review Insights**: Check your insights dashboard regularly to understand usage patterns
- **Keep Updated**: Stay current with new features and updates

Now that you're familiar with the basics, explore our other guides to learn about advanced features and use cases.
