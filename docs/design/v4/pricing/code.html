Strategic Assurance

Technical answers for high-stakes operational transitions.

Where is my data stored?

Data is hosted on sovereign Google Cloud nodes with localized residency options. For Enterprise clients, we offer specific geographic locking to meet regional compliance mandates (GDPR, CCPA, etc.).

How do I transition clients from Google Drive?

Our proprietary firmä-Migrate tool provides an automated, secure import pipeline. It mirrors your existing folder structures while applying our Zero-Knowledge security layer to every object.

Is there a per-seat charge?

No. We believe in growth alignment. We charge per client vault rather than per internal seat, allowing your entire team to collaborate without inflating your operational overhead as you expand your staff.
