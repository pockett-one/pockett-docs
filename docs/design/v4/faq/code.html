How secure is firmä with my Google Drive data?

firmä uses enterprise-grade OAuth 2.0 authentication. We never store your Google password. Your files remain on Google's servers; we only provide the portal interface to access and organize them according to your architectural workflows.

Can I invite clients to my firmä portal?

Yes. firmä is designed for collaboration. You can create private client areas where they can view specific project folders without seeing your entire internal directory.

Does firmä support automatic folder structures?

Absolutely. Our 'Velocity Templates' allow you to deploy standardized architectural folder structures across new projects with a single click, ensuring organizational consistency.

What happens if I cancel my subscription?

Your files are always yours. Since they live in your Google Drive, you retain full access to them through the native Google interface even if you decide to stop using firmä's architectural shell.
